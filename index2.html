How it began

Once upon a time, there was a lonely baby polar bear in Antartica. He had lost his parents after the house melted away, and he was in desperate search for a new home.

On the day of the biggest shooting star, the lonely baby polar bear wished for an icy shelter to keep him cold, soft creamy cushion with a ball pit for him to play in.

To his surprise, there was an icy cold mountain of soda cream cushion with cherry syrup streaks with popping candies!
